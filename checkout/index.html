Frequently Asked Questions

Is $29 really the full cost?

Yes. $29 one-time. No upsell required, no subscription, no hidden fees. You get full 2-day access and 7 days of replay for $29 total.

When is the event and what time zone is it in?

Exact dates and start times are confirmed at registration and in your confirmation email. Both days run live on Zoom, roughly 9am–4pm Central. If you can't make every minute live, replays post within 24 hours and stay up for 7 days after the event wraps.

What if I can't attend both days live?

We'd strongly recommend you do — the interactive Q&A is where most of the breakthroughs happen. But if you miss a session, replays stay available for 7 days after the event closes.

Will I get a workbook or any materials?

Yes. You'll get the 4-Pillar Framework workbook to fill out as you go, plus 7-day replay access after the event ends. Both come to the email you register with.

Who is this event NOT for?

If you're looking for a generic "how to start a business" webinar, this isn't it. Kingdom Scaling Principles is built for Christian business owners already doing $500K–$5M in revenue who feel trapped as the bottleneck in their own company.

What if I'm not quite at $500K in revenue yet?

If you're serious about scaling past where you are now, you'll still get value. The 4-Pillar framework works whether you're at $250K or $5M — the specific case studies and tactical focus skew toward the $500K–$5M range, but the operating principles apply at any size.

Will there be a pitch or upsell at the end?

Yes — we'll tell you about the next-level programs inside Rise Up Kings if you want to go deeper. But this event is complete on its own. You'll walk away with the full framework in 2 days whether you ever hear from us again or not. No gate, no "you have to upgrade to really get value."

What's the refund policy?

Show up to day one. If the framework doesn't deliver what we promised, email support@riseupkings.com before the event ends and we'll refund every dollar. No forms, no hoops.

What happens immediately after I pay?

You'll see a confirmation page and receive an email with your event access link, calendar invite, and a short welcome message from Skylar. You're in.

Is this safe? Where does my payment info go?

All payments are processed by Stripe — the same system trusted by Amazon, Shopify, and Target. Your card details never touch our servers. The connection is 256-bit SSL encrypted.
